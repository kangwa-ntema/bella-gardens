Fun Facts About Plants

There is a lot more to plants than many of us may know. Here are some interesting facts about plants you might have not known.

  • The average strawberry has 200 seeds. Its the only fruit that bears seeds on the outside.
  • There are over 300,000 different species of flowering plants in the world
  • Some flowers are capable of moving, a trait that is known as thigmotropism.
  • Dandelions might seem like weeds, but the flowers and leaves are a good source of vitamins A and C, iron, calcium and potassium. One cup of dandelion greens provides 7,000-13,000 I.U. of vitamin A.
  • Sunflowers move throughout the day in response to the movement of the sun from east to west.
  • The Bamboo tree can grow upto 35 inches a day.
  • More than 20% of the worlds oxygen supply is produced by the amazon rain forest.
  • The African tree, the Baobab can store 1,000 to 120,000 liters of water in its tree trunk.
  • Around 70,000 plants are been utilized around the world for medicine.
